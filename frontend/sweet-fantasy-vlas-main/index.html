About us

Sweet Fantasy began as a small dream of calm cups and gentle afternoons. Step inside — the room is quiet, light falls like linen, and on the ceiling, tiny bluebirds seem to glide as if borrowed from summer sky. We keep the hearth simple: careful Richard coffee and tea, playful bubble pearls, citrus lemonades, and pastries that recall Sunday kitchens — Napoleon layers, Zaharo treasures, brownies, cheesecake. Sit. Breathe. This is yours for a moment.
