Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

Sweet Fantasy uses customer information only to respond to cake orders, contact requests, and café enquiries.

Data We Collect

When you submit a cake order, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, preferred pickup date, cake preferences, and order notes.

How We Use Data

We use this data to confirm your order, contact you about details, and send order notifications to the Sweet Fantasy team. We do not sell personal data.

Email Processing

Order emails are sent through Resend. Your submitted order details are processed by Resend only for email delivery.

Cookies and Local Storage

The website uses essential local storage to remember language and cookie notice preferences. No advertising tracking is intentionally configured.

GDPR Rights

If you are in the EU, you may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data related to your order by contacting us.

Contact

For privacy questions, call +359 87 688 9232 or contact us through our Instagram profile.
